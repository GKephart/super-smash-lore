Purpose:

We would like this web app to help people find out more about the various characters in Smash Bros and the games they are individually in.

Audience:

The audience will be people of various ages and walks of life who play Smash Bros or video games as a whole.

Goal:

To educate and direct gamers wanting to know more about Smash and the characters in it.
