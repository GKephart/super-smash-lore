Male

25

Twitch/Streamer

Technology:

  • Nintendo Switch

  • Samsung Galaxy S10

Attitudes:

  • Tom is a Fanatic User who plays and live streams for a living.
  • Gaming is both a competitive sport and a Career to Tom Takahashi.
  • Tom's Competitive Nature compels him to succeed

Needs/Goals:

  • Tom has some expendable income being a famous streamer and wants to get into more games.
  • Tom wants to seek out the lore for each character and know more about them.
  • Tom needs to know which characters are worth having in his arsenal.

Frustrations:

  • Tom wants to play games for characters like Snake but doesnt know where to start.
  • Tom wants to know more about some of the characters he's never heard about before smash.
  • Tom wants to have the option of forming powerful character teams.

User Story

Tom will favorite specific characters in the website in order to have the option of refering back to them when it comes time to compete.

Use Case

Title

Tom wants to have new character knowledge at his fingertips in order to find new games for his off time.

Description

Tom will use his new found knowledge to find things to play when taking time off his competitive career, but add to his knowledge of the Smash games overall.

Precondition

Tom has his browser open and has a profile already made.

Postcondition

Tom is given information to related characters to add to his favorites list.

Interaction Flow

  • Tom enters the website into the browser
  • The server returns the website
  • Tom logs into his profile
  • The website displays his profile
  • Tom searches for Lucario
  • The website displays Lucarios information
  • He adds Lucario to his favorites

Alternatively

  • Tom enters the website into the browser
  • The server returns the website
  • Tom logs into his profile
  • The website displays his profile
  • Tom scrolls down and clicks on Lucario's icon
  • The website displays Lucarios information
  • He adds Lucario to his favorites
